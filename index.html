School Name

Achievements

ISA SPECIAL VIRTUAL ASSEMBLY ON 30th Sept,20

On 30th September 2020, a special virtual assembly was conducted by DWPS on the theme – ‘Less Plastic, More Life’ under the approval of the British Council’s International School Award (ISA). Various ISA activities like Paper and Cloth Bag Making, Group Song, Power Point Presentation, Slogan Writing and Speech were conducted during the special assembly. The students expressed themselves through different activities, spreading the message of creating an eco-friendly environment. Motivational speeches were given by the Project Head Ms. Anshu Gupta and ISA Coordinator Ms. Rashmi Sharma imparting knowledge to safeguard our ‘Mother Earth’.

What's New

For the first time in the State of Rajasthan, DWPS has collaborated with world's most celebrated cricket and Former Indian Captain, MS Dhoni to setup the first ever MS Dhoni Cricket Academy. The state of art cricket training facility will provide coaching using modern technology, high class coaching by national and international players under the mentorship of MS Dhoni.
